UPDATE ON OUR MAMA DELIVERY KITS
THANK YOU FOR YOUR SUPPORT!
DEAR READER,
We hope you and your loved ones are keeping safe. The entire mDoc team wishes to extend our gratitude to you for your donation and support which made the COVID19 Mama Delivery Kits project possible. Pregnant women and healthcare workers alike were delighted to receive the Mama Delivery Kits - made possible by your donations. Your donations are working to help prevent the transmission of COVID-19 and other hospital transmissible infections for over 400 health workers and mombaby pairs in Lagos State, Nigeria. We donated the Mama Delivery Kits at three healthcare facilities: Lagos University Teaching Hospital, Lagos Island Maternity Hospital and General Hospital Ikorodu.
Click here to watch our thank you video
FOR MOTHER AND BABY
Each pack of Mama Delivery Kit contained the following:
  • 3 Surgical masks
  • 1 Hand sanitizer
  • 1 Baby Blanket
  • AACE Foods Soya Maize
  • AACE food seasonings
FOR HEALTHCARE WORKERS,
Each pack of Mama Delivery Kit contained the following:
  • 1 pair of overalls
  • 1 Isolation gown
  • 1 Face shield
  • 1 FFP mask
  • 2 Sets of gloves
  • 1 pair of sleeve covers (for health workers who may be only have an apron)
  • 1 pair shoe covers

It will be very handy at this point in time when we are dealing with this pandemic because we know there are lots of issues on the shortage of PPE and we will always need any extra PPEs we can get at this point."
